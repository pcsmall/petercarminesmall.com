Background

I lead partnerships at Trove, powering circular commerce for brands like Arc'teryx, Carhartt, EILEEN FISHER, Levi's, Patagonia, and others. Before that, I scaled Beeline Bikes into the largest SaaS platform in the bike industry, bridging the gap between D2C brands and brick-and-mortar retailers in a category that had always run on traditional wholesale. Across 10+ years of startup experience, I've driven two successful exits, led multiple acquisitions and capital raises, and negotiated hundreds of brand and retail contracts. Earlier, I spent five years in merchandise planning and sourcing at Target.

Complex, cross-functional problems are where I'm most useful: scaling operations, structuring deals, and building things that haven't been built before.
